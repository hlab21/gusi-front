 GUSI is the College of Engineering, University of the East - Caloocan's digital repository. Its purpose is to showcase research materials — that is, unpublished researches — produced by the College of Engineering students of University of the East - Caloocan and make them more accessible to the students, faculty and admin of College of Engineering, University of the East - Caloocan.
To access GUSI, you only need to go to this link (coe.repository-uecal.com)
The admin will be the only one with access to upload your works. The professors will manage your work before turning it over to the administration.
