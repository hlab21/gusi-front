UE ECE Warrior Students Excel in Interschool Tilt!

Congratulations to the Bachelor of Science in Electronics Engineering (BSECE) students of the UE College of Engineering-Caloocan who won in various categories of the PalaIECEPan 2022! The competitions were held last October 13-15, 2022, at the Brgy. Poblacion Center covered court in Mandaluyong City, and via Zoom.

CEng’g Caloocan Warriors Win Championship, Shine at National Inter-school Convention!

A group of UE College of Engineering-Caloocan graduates and students of Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering (BSCE) shone bright at the recently held MIDAS Academic Festival!

Dubbed as the first convention of MIDAS academic users in the Philippines, the inter-school event was hosted by MIDAS IT Philippines and held last October 22, 2022, at Sequoia Hotel Manila Bay, and had participants from 29 universities and colleges nationwide.
